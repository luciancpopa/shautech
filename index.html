How we work

A four-phase engagement, defended on a single page.

Every project starts with a brief and ends with a handover. No surprises in the steering committee, no managed-service shaped exit.

Phase01

The brief

Two weeks. We sit with operators, read the audit reports, and write down what's true. You receive a current-state architecture and a position paper, in plain English.

Phase02

The blueprint

Target architecture, sequencing, risk register, fixed-fee delivery plan. Reviewed by a partner. Defendable to your board on a single page.

Phase03

The build

Senior engineers embedded with your team. Everything as code. Weekly demos, monthly stage gates — your CFO sees what they paid for.

Phase04

The handover

Runbooks, training, an honest exit plan. We don't reappear on Monday as a managed service line item — we're proud when you don't need us.

ShauTech is what an engagement should feel like — a small, senior team that arrived already knowing what was wrong, and left us better at running the platform than they were.
